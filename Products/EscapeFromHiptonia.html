Description

Escape From Hiptonia was created to test the concept of a survival RTS
game. The idea is that you crashed on a planet after having your ship
sabotaged by the oppressive hipsters who have taken over the global government
and is forcing everything man, woman, and child to wear neck beards.

Game Play

The game is started with one harvester and enough resources for
two turrets. Harvest more resources by left clicking on the harvester
and right clicking on the node to harvest from, the little blue squares.
The harvester will go back and forth harvesting and delivering its resources.
Once the resource is spent, the harvester will sit at the base until another
resource is selected. Turrets are placed by clicking on the turret button just
below the score and wave counters. Once you have enough resources, the
button will show up again allowing you to place additional turrets. Enemies
are spawned after each wave is defeated, and each wave contains an additional
number of enemies. The enemies will attack the nearest resource, turret, base,
or harvester until it is destroyed.

Scoring

Defeat as many enemies and enemy waves as you can. With the games current
settings, the highest wave I have been able to get to is 8.
