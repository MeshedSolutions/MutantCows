You play a kid who is always on the naughty list. Tired of being on the naughty list you decide that this year is going to be different. In the modern day and age of computers, Santa's list has to be stored on a computer due to its massive size. You decide to break into Santa's workshop and hack the list. Unfortunately, servers aren't the only upgrade at the north pole, with the latest in security and a small army of toys to stop you, hacking the list is going to be no easy task.
